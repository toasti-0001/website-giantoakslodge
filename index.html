About the lodge

Rustic at heart, more polished in the experience.

The original site leans into a warm brown palette, mountain imagery, and a simple family-lodge personality. This redesign keeps that identity while adding stronger typography, cleaner room browsing, better mobile spacing, and clearer booking paths.
